Veg Fry Supreme Recipe

Description

A great dish to make when there are left over vegetables/proteins in your fridge.

Waste not want not!

Ingredients

  • Any left over vegetables
  • Any left over proteins
  • Any carbs in your pantry
  • Love

Steps

  1. Fry up the vegetables until soft
  2. Put vegetables into a bowl
  3. Fry up the proteins
  4. Add vegetables and carbs to pan and continue frying
  5. Season and serve
