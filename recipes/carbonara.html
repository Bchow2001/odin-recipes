Carbonara Recipe

Description

Carbonara is an Italian dish originating from Rome. It is made with eggs, hard cheese, cured pork, and lots of black pepper.

It is one of my favourite dishes to make, being both flavourful, classy and most importantly of all easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 4 oz. Guanciale (soft cured prok jowl), Pancetta is an acceptable substitute, bacon is not
  • 2 oz.Pecorino Romano or Parmigiano Reggiano (similar mild hard cheeses are acceptable)
  • 4 Eggs, use an extra egg yolk for richness
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Spaghettoni or Spaghetti
