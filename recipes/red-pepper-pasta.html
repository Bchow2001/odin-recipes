Red Pepper Puree Pasta Recipe

Description

I learned this dish from my good friend Marta when she came over to visit me in Egham. She described the town I lived in for my Uni as "very boring" but I guarantee you that this dish is in no way boring.

I have made some changes of my own to suit my tastes but this is a very versatile dish that you can experiment with!

It is once again a very easy dish to make and more importantly can be made with only vegan ingredients for our more discerning friends.

Ingredients

  • 3 Large red peppers, diced finely
  • (Optional) Any sweet and hard vegetables
    • i.e. Carrots or parsley
  • Garlic, minced
  • 1 Large Onion, diced finely
  • Pasta
  • Olive oil
  • Paprika

Steps

  1. Boil salted water
  2. While water is boiling start frying the garlic and onion until soft
  3. Once soft, fry the pepper and other vegetables until very soft
  4. Add pasta to boiling water
  5. Blend vegetables using food processor until smooth
  6. Add paprika and other herbs of your choosing to blended sauce and blend some more
  7. Drain pasta but retain a little liquid, add sauce to pasta while heating on low heat
  8. Serve with black pepper and additional herbs
